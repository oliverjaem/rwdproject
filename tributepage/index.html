SB19

The Southeast Asia's Supergroup

Here's the timeline of SB19's Success

  • 2016 - SB19 was formed by ShowBT Philippines, the Philippine subsidiary of ShowBT Group in Korea.
  • 2018 - The group debuted with Josh, Pablo, Stell, Ken, and Justin on October 26, 2018, with the ballad single "Tilaluha".However, their initial efforts at promotions, mostly in schools and malls, were met with a "lukewarm reception".
  • 2019 - SB19's second single "Go Up" was released on July 19, 2019. Unexpectedly, they gained public attention after the dance practice video of the song was uploaded which was later shared by a fan on Twitter and Facebook.
  • 2020 - SB19 charted on four Billboard Charts and appeared at number six on Billboard Year-End Social 50 chart, making them the first Southeast Asian act to reach the top 10 of the magazine's annual chart.
  • 2021 - SB19 became the first Filipino and Southeast Asian act to be nominated in Billboard Music Awards for Top Social Artist. It marked the first-ever appearance of a Filipino artist in the Billboard Music Awards. The same year, SB19 has been nominated as Best Southeast Asia Act at the 2021 MTV Europe Music Awards
  • 2022 - SB19 was on the list of "The 100 Most Powerful People in the Philippines: Wielders of Soft Power" by the Esquire Magazine, the "Preview's 50 Most Influential for 2022" by Preview Magazine, and Teen Vogue’s ‘Favorite Boy Bands of All Time’ list.
  • In four years since debut, SB19 won 91 awards and 15 nominations, and still counting as they receive Local and International Nominations this 2022. They placed in six Billboard charts. Sold out Solo Concerts and breaking records. SB19 has become Southeast Asia's Supergroup.
  • SB19 is currently on their First Headline Tour (Where You At Tour) in Local and Intenational.
